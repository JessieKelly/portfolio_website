About

Yesh

Age: 11

Drummer, Keyboardist, Singer, Performer

I've always loved rhythm instruments. At 5 years old I started with the bongos and after a while I received a drum set from my parents. I play them when I do concerts and when I'm in the studio recording songs. On some songs I play piano. And lately, I am also really into coding and building computer games.

Evelyn

Age: 9

Keyboardist, Singer, Performer

I like beautiful things and I love to draw. I work with my brother on designing the characters for our computer games and when I perform with my family on stage, I sing, dance and play the piano. I was 5 days old, when my mother brought me on stage for the first time. For me it feels like home.
